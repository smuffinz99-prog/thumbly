YouTube thumbnail best practices

  • Use big, bold text. Your thumbnail is often seen at 168×94px on mobile. Text must be huge and readable at that size.
  • Keep it to 2–4 words. More text = smaller font = harder to read. Less is more.
  • High contrast backgrounds. Light text on dark backgrounds (or vice versa) always wins.
  • Add a face if you have one. Faces — especially expressing strong emotions — consistently outperform thumbnails without faces.
  • Stay consistent. Viewers recognise your channel faster when your thumbnails share a consistent style. Pro tip: pick one Thumbly template and stick to it.
