FAQ

Common questions

Is Thumbly free to use?
Yes — you can create and download 3 thumbnails per day completely free. No credit card, no account needed. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited downloads, no watermark, and all 14 templates.
What size are the downloaded thumbnails?
Every thumbnail downloads at exactly 1280×720 pixels in PNG format — the size YouTube recommends for thumbnails. There's no compression, so the quality is pixel-perfect.
Do I need to create an account?
No. Thumbly runs entirely in your browser. You don't need to sign up or give us your email address. Just open the editor and start making thumbnails.
Can I upload my own photo as the background?
Yes — click "Upload photo" in the editor and pick any JPG, PNG, or WEBP image from your device. Your photo is processed locally in your browser and is never uploaded to our servers.
What fonts are used?
Most templates use Impact or Arial Black — the same fonts used by the biggest YouTubers. These fonts are built into every operating system and browser, so no additional loading time.
How do I cancel Pro?
You can cancel Pro any time directly from your Stripe billing portal. No hoops to jump through — just one click. Your access continues until the end of the billing period.
